St. Mary's Coding Club

We create coders.

Our mission is to provide St. Mary's students the opportunity to graduate with a skill set and frame of mind that fosters innovation and collaboration to set them up for success in their future career. The absence of computer science at St. Mary's, even as an academic subject, leaves our students one step behind many other high schools in Calgary. Coding Club fills in the gap by inviting students to join a community of creators, collaborators and leaders.
